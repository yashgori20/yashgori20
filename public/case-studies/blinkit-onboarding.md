# Onboarding Flow - Blinkit [B2C Product]

Did you ever feel that if you could just magically get your groceries, household essentials delivered right to your doorstep without having to step a foot outside your house?

Worry not, all of us feel that way at some point or the other.

Be rest assured. Blinkit comes to your rescue!

*Imagine Salman Khan entering in a Dabangg Movie Franchise*. That’s right, Blinkit is the saviour to your rescue. One stop shop for all your needs!!!

A simple app from where you could get all your groceries, fruits, household essentials, cigarettes, party mixers [That’s right bachelors, talking about you] as well delivered right at your doorstep in under 10 minutes. Truly Magical, a god’s gift.

## Ideal Customer Profile:

1. Rushil
 - Rushil is a 23 year old bachelor, working in a remote job staying away from family and living in Pune with his college friends. He is a Computer Engineer by degree and currently working as a Full Stack Developer at a Blockchain based startup in HongKong.
 - Being a remote job, he does not have to commute to the office but he values time over money and needs the convenience of being able to get anything and everything delivered right to his doorstep.
 - He prefers to upskill himself in his free time by watching a couple of videos and in the remaining part step outside of the house to attend an event or travel on the weekend.
 - He uses various apps like
 - Social Media - Reddit, Youtube, Whatsapp, Instagram
 - News - Blind, TOI
 - Food Delivery - Zomato
 - OTT - Hotstar, Prime Video, Netflix
 - Grocery Delivery - Blinkit
 - Payments - Whatsapp Pay, Google Pay
 - For him his utmost priority is to have the convenience of getting groceries, snacks & fruits delivered right to his home when he needs it in the shortest amount of time possible. He never cooks at home, so his cook gives him the daily list of supplies needed and he ends up ordering daily thus being a power user of Blinkit.
 - Problem Statement for Rushil:
 - Rushil’s fridge is always empty when the cook comes over to cook and there is nothing present that can be used.
 - Also when he has some friends over at his place and he realizes he forgot to get the snacks in for everyone.
 - Thus he wants to be able to order everything instantly so all his needs can be met thereby valuing time over money.
 - JTBD for Rushil
 - Functional Goal [Primary] : Convenience, hassle free delivery right at the click of a few buttons
 - Personal Goal [Secondary] : To be able to make time for other activities like learning or socializing or stepping out of the house instead to go to a cafe with his friends.

 ## Overview of the JTBD
 
 Listed below is the combined user goals for Blinkit for both the ICP types which have been defined above
 
 1. Functional Goal :
 1. Convenience of getting groceries, snacks & household essentials delivered right to their doorstep without any hassle. Mainly helping the user save a considerable amount of time and the effort to instead go to a local vendor to get these items.
 2. Order right something as small as 1 Coca Cola Bottle or a bulk order for week long grocery supplies - Blinkit caters to all types of orders.
 2. Financial Goal : 
 1. Save money by getting access to plethora of discounts and offers [Bulk Buying, BNPL - Simpl. Credit Card Offers, Cred UPI discounts].
 3. Personal Goal:
 1. Make time for some other activities throughout the day instead of going grocery shopping to the local vendors.
 2. User can accommodate other activities during the day without having to worry about buying groceries, making list of the things needed & then actually going to the store to buy them.
 4. Social Goal:
 1. Helping everyone in a social or a group setting by placing orders and helping others get access to the items or things they needed instantly. Helps the users to flaunt a little on the grounds being able to help their loved ones.

## Activation Metrics

| Hypothesis | Reason |
| --- | --- |
| 1 successful order in the first 24 hours | The user needs to place an order in the first 24 hours of downloading the app to help them realize the core value proposition of the app. Else if a user only downloads the app and does not place an order, then these users will become churned users eventually. |
| 2 successful orders per week for the first 2 weeks. | This will help the ICP to realize the core value proposition of Blinkit, that is delivery of orders under 10 minutes. Also, having placed total 4 orders in 2 weeks means the app has successfully met all the user goals of the ICP and thus the user will come back to the app. |
| 9-10 successful orders in the first month. | This will help the team to analyze the user’s usage of the app and also gauge if the user will actually stick around later as well, given that they are becoming a power user of the app and have started to rely on it [blinkit] to get their needs fulfillled. |
| Avail Discounts via Credit Card Offers, BNPL services like Simpl or UPI enabled services [Cred UPI]. | The AOV for the user interviews I conducted was around INR 300-350, thus not making them eligible instantly for the discounts.
