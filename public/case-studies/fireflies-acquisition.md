### David, The Visionary Founder (Power User)

---

Meet David, a 33-year-old founder with a track record of two successful startups under his belt. He has recently secured a seed round for his second venture and is laser-focused on propelling it to greater heights. With a staggering 7 hours spent in meetings every day, spanning internal and external discussions across various business verticals, David needed a tool that could match his ambition.

The challenge was clear: how to consume copious amounts of information, have easy access to meeting notes for later reference, and efficiently track action items, all while staying clear of the overwhelming whirlpool of manual note-taking.

David heard about Fireflies.ai through a fellow founder at Y Combinator. The recommendation was intriguing, and he was drawn to it like a moth to a flame. Before Fireflies.ai, managing discussions and decisions in the tempestuous sea of startup leadership was a Herculean task. It was essential but daunting, like navigating a ship through a storm without a compass.

But Fireflies.ai changed everything. It became David's trusted sidekick in the world of leadership meetings and strategic decisions. The platform ensured that key decisions were captured, and action items were diligently tracked. It was the anchor that kept him steady in the stormy sea of meetings. He drove the top management at his startup to embrace Fireflies.ai in their daily meetings, knowing that it could be the compass they needed.

### Sarah, The Project Manager (Lost User)

---

Meet Sarah, a 33-year-old Cloud Computing enthusiast and Project Manager at Ant Stack. With over 2 years of orchestrating success projects under her belt, her daily routine was a whirlwind of responsibilities. Her role was to ensure client satisfaction, internal project synchronization, and steer the ship through two weekly client meetings and multiple internal meetings.

The journey of Sarah with Fireflies.ai began with a recommendation from the CEO. The nature of her work, which involved back-to-back meetings that constituted 40-45% of her daily grind, demanded an efficient solution. Fireflies.ai fit the bill perfectly. It transcribed and summarized these marathon meetings, simplifying her life and helping her pinpoint key action items. The platform's proficiency in overcoming language barriers was a blessing for her globally distributed team.

As communication with her clients and teams became more seamless, Sarah gradually reduced her reliance on Fireflies.ai, but it remained her trusted companion for new client meetings. In her journey with Fireflies.ai, Sarah found call summarization and key action items to be the most beneficial features. They streamlined her meetings and post-meeting reviews.

As AI caught wind, Zoom started with summarizing their meetings and Fireflies.ai lost its love in the heart of Sarah. But Sarah had valuable insights to share. A summary email post-meeting was on her wish list, along with an admission that she hadn't explored the voice feature. She believes that could have changed her outlook for the product, but never delved deeper into the platform itself. Her parting wish was for better integration, making Fireflies.ai an even more seamless part of her daily routine.

### Rebecca, The Sales Machine (Power User)

---

With a Bachelors's degree in Commerce and 1.5 years of professional experience, Rebecca is no stranger to the world of business. She is a formidable force in the Revenue Team at Hevo Data, a B2B SaaS software company with over 250 employees, generating an impressive annual revenue of $10M-$15M.

Becky is responsible for US Mid Market (E2E Sales), which means she's in the thick of things when it comes to generating demand and closing deals. Her typical workday is a whirlwind of activities. It starts with a team cadence, followed by emails and preparations for the day. The heart of her day is lead generation and client calls. In her role, she interacts with clients, team leads, and works across locations.

Rebecca's journey with Fireflies.ai began through her brother's recommendation. Her initial experience with Fireflies.ai was during her tenure at Pepper Content. She was so impressed with its capabilities that she started using it individually and later got her subscription through the company. The familiarity with Fireflies.ai was her motivation to continue using it when she transitioned to Hevo Data.

In her daily routine, Fireflies.ai plays a pivotal role in her client meetings. With the client's permission, it's a "must" tool for Rebecca. She doesn't stop there; she also uses it for internal team meetings, especially for operations discussions and meetings with team leads. At Hevo, she leverages the Google integration, and at Pepper Content, Salesforce integration was her go to, allowing her to log calls and reduce manual tasks. She does note that for some platforms like Microsoft, there are integration challenges that need addressing.

Collaboration with Fireflies.ai is a breeze for Rebecca. She uses it for personal use and only shares meeting notes when asked for. She picks out key points for logging in HubSpot, uses voice recording as a backup when she forgets to take manual notes, and appreciates having all meeting information in one place to collate action items.

Fireflies.ai is in its stages of early scaling where it is important to try multiple channels of acquisition. Currently, 90% Fireflies.ai's traffic comes from direct and organic search - which is good as they are high generally high intent buyers who know the product and the likelihood of converting them into a customer is high. This also means there is an opportunity to improve the conversion rate of these leads by serving them relevant content. But also, a bigger opportunity lies in exploring more of the others channels. Being in early stages of scaling, **speed, scale, effort and CAC** play an important role. While continuing to focus on Organic Search which they are doing well, Fireflies.ai can focus on Content Loop and Partner Program to drive acquisition of high intent users.

## Partner Program

---

Being a meeting assistant for the high meeting frequency persona, Fireflies.ai has found it's preferred customers in the sales persona (Rebecca) and project manager persona (David) and continuing to double down on targeting these personas. Fireflies.ai can develop and run a partner program with Sales Training and Enablement companies specifically. Working with these companies Fireflies.ai can get their platform involved in the training program and give these high intent users a quick taste of the platform.

**Program Benefits for Partners:**

1. **Comprehensive Training:** Partners receive access to in-depth training modules covering Fireflies.ai's features, benefits, and use cases.
2. **Sales Enablement:** Partners are equipped with sales collateral, resources, and tools to effectively communicate the value of Fireflies.ai to potential clients.
3. **Dedicated Support:** Partners have access to a dedicated support team to assist with any product-related inquiries or customer issues.
4. **Co-Marketing Opportunities:** Partners can collaborate with Fireflies.ai on joint marketing efforts, including webinars, content creation, and case studies.
5. **Additional Revenue Source:** The partners can earn a commission on driving sales to their customers who would have a relevant use case. Furthermore building out stronger relationship these partners can include the access to the platform in their cost

## Draft Proposal for Partner Program

### 1. Objective

- Accelerate adoption among **high-meeting-frequency personas** (Sales, Project Management, Leadership) through **distribution partnerships**.
- Position Fireflies.ai as a default meeting assistant inside **training, enablement, and productivity ecosystems**.
- Reduce reliance on pure organic/direct traffic by embedding into partner-led workflows.

---

### 2. Partner Categories

1. **Sales Training & Enablement Firms**
 - Use Fireflies as a core tool in rep onboarding and call-coaching.
 - Example partners: Sandler, Winning by Design, SaaS Academy.
2. **Project Management Consultancies**
 - Bundle Fireflies for knowledge capture in Agile/PM transformations.
3. **Tech Platforms & Productivity Tools (Integrations as Partners)**
 - Deeper integrations with CRM (Salesforce, HubSpot), PM tools (Jira, Asana, Monday), and LMS platforms.
4. **Enterprise SaaS Resellers / VARs**
 - Channel partners reselling productivity suites (Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zoom ecosystem).

---

### 3. Partner Tiers

| **Tier** | **Criteria** | **Benefits** | **Fireflies Commitment** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Bronze** | Referral-based | 10% revenue share, free training | Dedicated referral portal, credits |
| **Silver** | 20+ paying customers onboarded | 15% revenue share, early feature access | Co-branded content, partner Slack channel |
| **Gold** | 100+ paying customers OR embedded Fireflies in their training/product | 20% revenue share, API/SDK support | Joint GTM (webinars, case studies), premium support |

---

## 4. Platform Integration Layer

- **Partner Portal (Web + Mobile + Chrome extension hooks):**
 - Unified dashboard for referrals, co-marketing assets, analytics.
 - Push notifications for leads, payouts, new features.
- **Partner-Facing SDK / APIs:**
 - Allow training firms or SaaS partners to embed Fireflies meeting notes into their own platform.
 - Example: A sales enablement tool can show “Fireflies Summary + Action Items” inside their dashboards.
- **Lifecycle Hooks:**
 - Partner admins can auto enroll cohorts (new sales reps, PMs) with templated onboarding.
 - Push notifications + in-app nudges (desktop/mobile/extension) to boost first-call activation.

---

## 5. Incentives & Monetization

- **Revenue Share:** 10-20% depending on tier.
- **Non-Monetary Incentives:** Co-branded webinars, case studies, certifications (“Fireflies Certified Partner”).
- **Access Incentives:** Priority integrations, early access to real-time features (voice AI, live summaries).

---

## 6. GTM Playbook

1. **Recruit**
 - Target training firms + PM consultancies in North America (largest SAM share).
 - Offer free trials bundled into their programs.
2. **Enable**
 - Partner LMS: short training modules + certifications.
 - Use Fireflies desktop/mobile push notifications for onboarding triggers.
3. **Activate**
 - Auto-invite users into Fireflies via Chrome extension or meeting calendar integration.
 - Nudges: “See your first AI summary in < 1 hour.”
4. **Retain & Expand**
 - Shared dashboards for partners showing adoption metrics.
 - Revenue payouts linked to retention (not just sign-ups).

---

## 7. Success Metrics

- **Partner-Sourced ARR %** (goal: 10-15% of new ARR in 12 months).
- **Partner Activation Rate** (partners who onboard ≥20 users within first 60 days).
- **User Retention Lift** (compare partner-led cohorts vs organic signups).
- **Integration Depth** (number of partner platforms embedding Fireflies summaries).

---

## 8. Differentiation vs Competitors (Zoom, Otter.ai, etc.)

- Zoom native AI is **single-platform only**; Fireflies cross-platform + embeddable SDK = stronger partner fit.
- Otter.ai lacks structured partner tiers and revenue incentives.
- Fireflies can own the **“AI note taking infra layer”** inside sales/PM training ecosystems

---

## 9. Why This Matters to Fireflies

- **Adoption**: partners auto enroll cohorts → instant activation.
- **Retention**: partner led use cases keep Fireflies sticky inside workflows.
- **Scale**: partners unlock access to hundreds of reps/managers at once.

## Referral Program

---

Fireflies.ai has an active referral program that has the following features:

- A separate window to refer that gives different modes to share
- A focused list with recommendations based on previous meeting attendees
- "Access" is the platform currency and unlocks additional features or extra credits for these features
- A referral tracker

They have a decent referral program in place, that works well for attracting individual users along with a freemium option. But it is not an enticing offering for individuals to refer as these credits perhaps don't mean much. A month of premium access to both referrer and referee could still work well.

Being a B2B SaaS tool, the impact of individual referral is limited and would work well if its done at team/organization level where the company can pay for the premium membership and hence I decided to focus on Partner Program where I see more focus and value that can be delivered at scale.
