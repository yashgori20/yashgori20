# Acquisition Strategy - Jio Cinema Premium [B2C Product]

Jio Cinema is an India OTT Streaming platform owned by Viacom 18. They Provide Live Streaming of Sports and VOD as well. It is currently operating in Advertising (AVOD) and Subscription (SVOD) models. It was launched in the year 2016 as part of Jio Platforms Ltd. After the recent acquisition of IPL 2023 rights, the platform was integrated with Viacom18, Bodhi Tree Systems and Paramount Global. August 2023, the content library of Voot (Previously owned by Viacom18) has been moved to Jio Cinema. This content library has made Jio Cinema one of the prime OTT platforms in the country. 

- **Available Languages**: English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Marathi, Gujarati, Bengali and Bhojpuri.
- **Content Rights:** Contents of Colors Viacom 18, HBO, Warner Bros and Jio Studios
- **Sports Rights:** Tata IPL - 2023 - 2027, Ligue1, La Liga, Moto GP, Serie A, BWF, MLC, SAT20, Olympics2024, NBA
- **Core Value Proposition**
 - Stream Sports and Movies in the desired language of the users at any time and anywhere.

| Referral | Low | Low | Medium | High | Medium |

| Product Integrations | High | Low | Medium | low | Low |
| Affiliate Marketing | High | Medium | Low | medium | High |

**Referrals**: Though a lot of traffic comes from SEO and paid Ads. The Referral programs will provide the much-needed boost in acquiring new quality users. JioCinema Premium has been facing issues related to a lack of quality user sign-ups. The referrals will help the platform get long-term loyal Subscribers.

**Paid Ads:** The Paid Ads Approach can effectively reach out to potential users about the upcoming titles in JioCinema Premium. Paid Ads will also allow the user to experience the platform and Access the content free of cost for 1 month.

**Product Integration:** To increase user engagement, it is critical to identify and target relevant products/services that can enhance the content-consuming experience for users. By doing so, it can attract quality subscribers who are looking for premium and international content.

- **Referral Acquisition**
 - What does the user brag about for JioCinema Premium :
 1. Watching new series/movie
 2. Enjoying unlimited access to movies/series and premium sports content
 3. Finding exclusive content only available in JioCinema Premium
 
 - **Platform Currency**
 
 The platform Currency is Subscription and Early Access that can be granted for their favourite shows for each referral.
 
 - **Referral Process**
 - **Option 1:** Existing Subscribers consuming Content → He follows and binge watches shows and movies→ Asking for Rating the Content Consumed→ If the User provides positive Feedback → Pop appears asking to refer friends and family to enjoy the series/ movies with the incentive of Additional 15 days subscription for each referral
 - **Option 2:** User consuming content → He follows and binge watches shows and movies → Asking for Rating the content Consumed → If the User provides positive Feedback - Pop up Appears “To Enjoy the early access of your favourite show refer and sign up 5 of your friends and families” “To Enjoy the early access for all the JioCinema premium content by 24 hrs for next 3 Months refer and sign up 10 of your friends and families”
 - **Tracking Referrals**
 
 Users can refer to one or too many during the referral cycle. Each Referral will have a unique coupon code for the new user to use during sign-ups. The existing user can check on the Reward page about the referral status and if he is eligible for an Extended subscription or early access
 
 - **How to continue Referrals**
 - Incentivise the user with different Referral Slabs
 - 1 Referral - 15 days additional subscription
 - 3 Referrals - 1 Month Additional Subscription / 1 Movie/Episode early access
 - 5 Referrals - 45 days Additional subscription / 2 Movies / 3 Episodes early access
 - 10 Referrals - 3 Months additional subscription / 3 Months early access for all the contents

 **Rent a Movie with JioCinema Premium:** Integrating JioCinema in BookMyshow App’s Steam Page. Jio Cinema will be part of the BMS stream page where users can rent a movie from JioCinema for One time watch. This will help both the platform to acquire new users. BMS will get new users to try their steaming page, and this will help JioCinema to get new users to sign up and experience the premium content in the App.
 
 - **BookMyShow**
 - Rent a Movie with JioCinema Premium
 - Bookmyshow streams movies on a Rental Basis for limited steaming time. JioCinema Premium movies can be rented via Bookmyshow. Users who rent the movies will get a chance to win 15 days of JioCinema Premium Subscription
 - What is in it for Bookmyshow
 - This will increase the titles on the Bookmyshow streaming page. More traffic will enable BMS to attract more users to the platform.
 - What is in for JioCinema Premium
 - Users get used to experiencing JioCinema Premium content. This will enable users to explore more content in JioCinema Premium and push the users to subscribe to JioCinema Premium at a subsidized cost with the BMS activation link.

 - **Boat Speakers/headphones -**
 - **Experience Crystal clear sound with Exclusive JioCinema Premium Boat headphones:**
 - Special JioCinema Premium Boat Headphones priced above 3000 to be available on Boat websites/Amazon. For every purchase of the special edition of headphones, users get 3 Months of Free Subscription for Jio Cinema Premium and 1 Early Access to Movie streaming in JioCinema Premium.
 - What is in it for Boat
 - The special edition of JioCinema Premium Headphones will help position itself as an aspirational brand, especially for ICP 1 users.
 - This will help Boat increase the sale of headphones as there is an incentive of subscription for the users on purchase of Boat headphones.
 - What is in it for JioCinema Premium:
 - Associating with Jio Cinema will help both Boat Headphones and JioCinema to acquire new ones. This will also help JioCinema to position itself as a Premium streaming platform with Crystal Clear audio. Once this is successful, JioCinema can tie up with other headphones and Projector brands.
