 ### ‼ Applying the ICP Prioritization Framework
 
 | | Agencies | SME | Startups | Enterprise |
 | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
 | Adoption Curve | High | High | High | Medium |
 | Frequency of Use | High | High | Medium | Low |
 | Appetite to Pay | Low | Medium | Medium | High |
 | Distribution Potential | High | High | High | High |
 | TAM | Medium | High | High | Medium |
 
 Basis on the above ICP’s by using the ICP Prioritization Framework, I have prioritized Startups and SME’s since the developers working in these org’s have a very frequent usage of vercel due to rapid prototyping and development thus helping them with their Go To Market strategies. Vercel precisely solves the needs of these developers who are looking for a streamlined experience to manage their entire cloud infrastructure without having to worry about unpredictable traffic.
 
 Enterprises do not generally go for rapid prototyping and deployment as they have a longer development cycle due to extensive testing and maintenance. Also, since they have a devops team, often they need a cloud service which can be completely managed by their “Terminal” and they won’t have to rely on someone.
 
 Vercel has a pricing plan with per seat being chargeable. Quite unlike the pricing of AWS where you pay for what you use. [In vercel also there are certain thresholds and once that is crossed then the user has to pay for the extra services they have consumed.] This pricing is affordable enough for moderately sized teams and not enterprises. To tackle this, Vercel introduced on Enterprise plan for 3k USD per month where it gives 300 seats to the company thereby effectively charging only 10 USD per seat per month [Original fixed pricing is 20 USD per seat per month].

Thus Paids Ads as a channel will not be used for this assignment.

## Deep Dive into Acquisition Channels

### 1. Content Loops

The main goal of content loops would be to educate the ICP’s with the numerous use cases of vercel and to cement Vercel as an industry standard as a cloud deployment platform. Currently vercel hires “Product Advocates” who attend conferences, webinars and post content on communities inorder to increase awareness about Vercel. We can work on Content Loops as a channel while diving more deep into the same strategy being employed currently by them.

- What kind of content can we produce that users will share?
 - Content regarding general tech stacks being used nowadays and how vercel solves those problems.
 - Use-cases based content regarding how to effectively deploy frontend apps.
 - Useful resources in git repositories.

- Will users create content for you? If yes, how will you distribute it?
 - Git repositories can be open sourced. Anyone who finds it useful can contribute
 - Tech writer pages to nudge people to contribute ([Digital Ocean Model](https://www.digitalocean.com/community/pages/write-for-digitalocean))
 - Distribution Channels: Dev.to, Reddit, Snippets on Twitter, Youtube Videos and Shorts, Linkedin
 - Main Driver for distribution: Google SEO [will inadvertently help in SEO ranking and vercel’ SERP as well]
- How can you get users to create and share content?
 - Opensource repositories
 - Provide space for debate & discussion in communities
 - Developers can commit to the open source repo of Next.js [Founder of Vercel also founded the framework Next.js which is predominantly used for any application to be deployed on vercel] as well

### 2. Partner Program

Partner Program has always been one of the most important means to on-board users for Vercel, since once a dev realized the core value proposition of Vercel they can help other devs to realized that core value proposition again as well. 

The current partner program for Vercel focusses only on Agencies where the Agency has to submit an application and the Vercel team will review it and get back on the status. This same program can be extended to other paying customers as well inorder to help Vercel build a more mature ecosystem and acquire more users.

- 🆕 **Define Bragworthy things?**
 1. Instant Deployment and Scalability
 2. Serverless Functions Integration
 3. Developer friendly workflow and collaboration
- **Platform Currency**
 1. Discount on Subscription- Refer to your colleagues and get x% discount [Vercel’s current pricing plan is on basis of per seat, but we cannot give free seats as a platform currency as in some cases the referee might have a very high ACV and thus the referrer will always get by with their free seats thereby leading to loss in revenue for Vercel]
 2. Get access to live 24x7 customer support.
- **Who will you ask for a referral?**
 1. Users who have been onboarded and have made the first deployment.
 2. Users who used have used it for a month and paid for their subscription amount.
 3. Users who have given a positive review on the CSAT survey.

 **User who have made the first subscription payment after being onboarded.**

 - Users clicks to refer

 - The referee subscribes to Vercel using the link shared
 - The referrer gets a discount on his email and also gets live technical support enabled for his organization account.

 There is an entirely different dashboard [vercel.com/partners] where the referrers can go ahead and track the referrals.
